Engin Dogusoy

I design and develop software to fund traveling, comic books and quality coffee.Enjoy building products that are used everyday and working in small teams of smart, communicative people. Oh, also ice cream.

Born and raised in Istanbul, Turkey. Moved to Bay Area, California in 2010 to get a Master's Degree from Carnegie Mellon University in Software Engineering. Joined SAP Labs, Palo Alto prototyping team after graduation to work on cloud infrastructures and geo-location applications for 2 years. Working for Fleksy, San Francisco with a group of amazing people since November 2013.

I spend most of my time working on Fleksy Android apps. I am also responsible of Fleksy Cloud, a cloud backend that syncs user specific information across various Android stores. Having many toys around is one of the greatest benefits of working in an innovative startup. We have all kinds of game pads, gesture detection systems and wearables lying around the office and every now then I try to build something cool with them.

I adore meticulously designed and well documented APIs and always try to improve my design skills with personal projects. There is no better gift than a beautiful API for a developer.

Enjoy snowboarding, traveling and a sunny afternoon. Always on the lookout to improve my taste in music and photography.
